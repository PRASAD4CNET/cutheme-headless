Sprott Employability Passport

BCom ONLY

Students in the Employability Passport are responsible for their enrolment and progression in their Employability Passport courses (BUSI 1995, BUSI 2995, BUSI 3995, and BUSI 4995). Please make sure to confirm your current standing and register in the appropriate level courses. Please connect with the Employability Passport Coordinator if you have any questions about your progression at Employability.Passport@carleton.ca.

The successful completion of the Sprott Employability Passport courses
(BUSI 1995, BUSI 2995, BUSI 3995, and BUSI 4995) are graduation requirements for Sprott Bachelor of Commerce students admitted into first-year for Fall 2019 and beyond.
