Job Summary

The Dream Legacy Foundation (DLF) and Carleton University Sprott School of Business and Black Entrepreneurship Knowledge Hub (BEKH) is a critical national network of research hubs involved in community-based research, with a mission to improve the lived experiences of the Black community. Through collaboration with a network of multidisciplinary researchers and in linking these with community-based researchers and practitioners and by adopting a regional community-based strategy, the BEKH will co-create and co-generate research that will deliver important knowledge, insights and outcomes that resonates with the local regional needs of Black entrepreneurs, community members and organizations across Canada.

To accomplish this important mission, the BEKH seeks applications for the position of Media and Communications Coordinator (Research) to help promote the activities of academic and community-based researchers and associated activities of the BEKH and its network of Regional Hubs and community partners. Reporting to the BEKH Research Program Manager, the Media and Communications Coordinator (Research) is responsible for maintaining and updating all aspects of the BEKH’s website and social media presence, including content creation.

The Media and Communications Coordinator (Research) will be based in the Central Hub in Carleton University’s Innovation Hub and will work closely with the Dream Legacy Foundation (DLF), the BEKH Directors, Regional Hub leads, the Sprott School of Business, and Innovation Hub staff to ensure that the BEKH fulfils its national mandate and objectives and its commitment to Black entrepreneurs, community members and organizations across Canada. This position will work with BEKH leadership, Regional Hubs, and researchers to develop and implement a National Communication strategy and appropriate social media campaigns. They will develop and advance communications skills in content creation, social media, news story writing, interviewing, photography, and videography, and will contribute to internal and external engagement of the BEKH, its network of partners and the Black community.

Key Accountabilities

Website

  • develops, designs, and maintains the BEKH’s website and contributes to the ongoing enhancement of BEKH’s communications through the creation of originally designed visual and interactive content, compelling graphic design, photography, audio production and videography
  • writes and posts content - research project descriptions, Regional Hub profiles, researcher and community partner profiles, news articles, success stories and other content, promotes events and conferences which support the BEKH’s strategic priorities, events, and activities
  • conducts interviews with researchers and community members across all regional hubs to provide content for website and news items
  • captures and/or aggregates photography and video related to BEKH stories and events for communications across platforms
  • ensures all information is up to date and new information is posted on a timely basis
  • ensures that the website complies with university rules and regulations and is accessible to community-based audiences
  • ensures that links are current (not broken)
  • works collaboratively with Information Technology Services (ITS) on any upgrades
  • works collaboratively with researchers and community members across all hubs in the network to implement new pages on the website, registration forms, or other changes
  • analyses website performance using tools such as Google Analytics

BEKH Social Media Accounts

  • maintains the BEKH’s social media accounts - Twitter, LinkedIn, Tik Tok, Instagram, Facebook, blogs, podcasts, and others
  • produces video, photo, and audio content designed exclusively to best tell BEKH’s stories through digital social media campaigns
  • creates social media campaigns and visual material to promote BEKH’s research, stories, publications, and events and monitors comments
  • works collaboratively with researchers and community members across all hubs in the network to plan and coordinate social media campaigns
  • reports on the effectiveness of BEKH digital campaigns using social media analytics and metrics and makes necessary adjustments to social media strategies
  • ensures compliance with university standards for social media
  • investigates the use of new social media platforms
  • assists BEKH Directors with social media posts, blogs or podcasts as required

Project Planning and Management

  • works to develop national branding for the BEKH
  • works with the DLF, the BEKH and its Regional Hubs to ensure a timely development of web and social media project deliverables
  • establishes web and social media project schedules, timelines, milestones, contracts, and resources
  • supports event local and regional event planning
  • identifies and facilitates the translation of the website and key social media posts into French

Impact

  • contributes to reputation and public images of the BEKH, DLF, Carleton University and the Sprott School of Business
