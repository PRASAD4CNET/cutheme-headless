Faculty Writing Workshop

Winter 2022 - Silent and Virtual Writing Workshop

When: Monday-Friday from 10:00 AM-12:00 PM throughout the winter term, beginning the week of January 10th.

Where: Zoom (Click here to register)

This term, participating faculty will moderate the virtual writing workshop themselves. 

Participants will write for 25-minute intervals, followed by 5-minute breaks.

A Pomodoro-style timer can be accessed here: https://pomofocus.io/

Please contact Alyssa Tremblay (alyssatremblay@cunet.carleton.ca) if you encounter any technical issues.

Happy writing! ✏️
